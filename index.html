Who We Are

Sport Aids Africa(SAA) is a Kenyan
Non-Governmental Organisation. We are dedicated to
serving the forcibly displaced persons residing in Dadaab,
Kakuma and Kalobeyei refugee camp.

While watching ALJAZEERA television one
Friday early in 2010, Bernard Bitter saw a documentary
on the dabaab refugee camp in Kenya

At the time Dabaab was the largest refugee campin the
world with approximately 500,000 refugees. Bernard was
compelled to do something about the plight of refugees.
This led to the establishment of Sports Aid Africa in
August 2012, as a vehicle to bring hope to refugees in
camps.

OUR OBJECTIVES

- To identify the needs in the campus and present them
to donor partners.
- To partner with like-minded organizations as the
implementing partner in helping them fulfil their
objective to the community.
- To conduct sports activities for all age groups in the
refugee camp and local communities.
- To provide recreation and leadership development
training.
- To provide multipurpose sports facilities.
- To provide fixed and consumable sports equipment
and resources, with a goal of building a self-sustainable
aid organisation.
